Fast Korean Beef Quesadillas with Sesame Cucumber Salad & Gochujang Mayo

Cuisine: Korean-Mexican Fusion

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add ground beef, garlic, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Cook until the beef is browned and the sauce has thickened.
  2. In a bowl, combine sliced cucumber, rice vinegar, and sesame seeds. Toss to coat and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, mix gochujang and mayonnaise to create the gochujang mayo. Set aside.
  4. Lay a tortilla on a flat surface, sprinkle cheese on half, add a layer of cooked beef, then fold the tortilla over. Repeat with the remaining tortillas.
  5. Cook the quesadillas in a dry skillet over medium heat until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  6. Slice the quesadillas into wedges and serve with the cucumber salad and gochujang mayo on the side. Enjoy!
