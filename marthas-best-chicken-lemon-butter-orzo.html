Martha’s Best Chicken & Lemon Butter Orzo with Spinach & Parmesan

Cuisine: Mediterranean

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup orzo pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2 cups spinach (fresh)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and sear chicken until golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, melt butter and add orzo. Stir until lightly toasted.
  3. Add chicken broth, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet, cover, and cook until the chicken is fully cooked and the orzo is tender, about 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in spinach and Parmesan cheese until the spinach is wilted and the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
