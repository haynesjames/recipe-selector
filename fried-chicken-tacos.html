Fried Chicken Tacos with Charred Corn Salsa & Chipotle Mayo

Cuisine: Mexican Fusion

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper, cut into ¼-inch pieces
  • 1 oz scallions, thinly sliced
  • ¼ oz fresh cilantro, coarsely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced (plus wedges for serving)
  • 5 oz corn
  • 16 oz boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 (6-inch) corn tortillas
  • 4 oz guacamole
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (plus more for frying)
  • Kosher salt and ground pepper

Chipotle Mayo

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a small bowl until smooth. Adjust seasoning to taste and set aside.

Instructions

  1. Prep Ingredients: Halve the bell pepper, remove the stem and seeds, then cut into ¼-inch pieces. Trim scallions, then thinly slice. Coarsely chop cilantro. Squeeze 1 tbsp lime juice into a medium bowl and whisk with 1 tbsp olive oil. Cut any remaining lime into wedges.
  2. Cook Corn: Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large heavy skillet over high heat. Add corn and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender and lightly charred, about 5 minutes. Transfer to the bowl with lime juice and set aside to cool. Wipe out the skillet for later use.
  3. Prep Chicken: Pat the chicken dry and cut crosswise into 1-inch wide strips. In a medium bowl, combine ½ cup flour and ½ cup water; season with salt and pepper. Add the chicken to the batter and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Toast Tortillas: Carefully toast tortillas over an open gas flame until lightly charred in spots, about 5–10 seconds per side. Alternatively, preheat the broiler and broil tortillas in batches until lightly charred. Wrap in foil to keep warm.
  5. Cook Chicken: Heat ¼ inch of oil in the skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Add chicken strips and cook, turning once, until golden and crisp, about 6–9 minutes total. Use a slotted spoon to transfer chicken to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
  6. Finish & Serve: Add scallions, peppers, and half of the cilantro to the corn. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Spread some chipotle mayo onto each tortilla, then top with chicken, corn salsa, a dollop of guacamole, and a sprinkle of the remaining cilantro. Serve with lime wedges and extra chipotle mayo. Enjoy!
