Chicken Piccata

Cuisine: Italian

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh lemon
  • ¼ bunch Italian parsley
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2–3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 2 lbs)
  • Pinch salt and pepper
  • ½ cup flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp capers

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Roughly chop parsley leaves, juice the lemon, and mince the garlic. Set aside.
  2. Prepare Chicken: Trim excess fat from chicken breasts. Cover with plastic wrap and pound to an even thickness (about ½–¾ inch). Cut each breast in half to make 4–6 pieces. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Brown Chicken: Heat olive oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Coat chicken pieces lightly in flour. Once the oil is hot but not smoking, add chicken and cook until golden brown on both sides (about 3–5 minutes per side). Cook in batches to avoid overcrowding. Transfer browned chicken to a clean plate.
  4. Make Sauce: Reduce heat to medium-low. Add butter and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté garlic for about 1 minute. Add chicken broth and lemon juice, whisking to deglaze the pan and dissolve browned bits.
  5. Finish Sauce: Add capers and half of the chopped parsley to the skillet. Increase heat to medium and return chicken to the pan. Spoon sauce over chicken and let simmer without a lid for 3–5 minutes, or until sauce reduces by half.
  6. Serve: Sprinkle remaining parsley over the chicken and serve immediately. Enjoy!
