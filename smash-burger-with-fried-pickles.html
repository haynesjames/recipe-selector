Smash Burger with Fried Pickles and Fries

Cuisine: American

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes, scrubbed and cut into ½-inch thick fries
  • 1½ oz cornstarch (about 3 tbsp)
  • 1 red onion, halved (½ thinly sliced, ½ reserved for another use)
  • 3¼ oz dill pickles, sliced
  • 1 romaine heart, separated into leaves
  • 3 tbsp panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 potato buns
  • 16 oz ground beef
  • 1½ oz ranch dressing (about 3 tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp steak seasoning
  • 1 large egg
  • Neutral oil
  • Kosher salt and ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Prep Fries: Preheat the oven to 450°F with a rack in the bottom position. On a rimmed baking sheet, toss the potato fries with 1 tbsp cornstarch, 2 tsp oil, salt, and pepper. Spread in a single layer.
  2. Bake Fries: Roast the potatoes on the bottom oven rack until tender and browned on the bottom, about 20–25 minutes. Flip fries and cook until crispy, about 10 minutes more. Meanwhile, thinly slice ½ of the red onion, pour pickle brine into a small bowl, and pat pickle slices dry. Halve lettuce leaves crosswise.
  3. Fry Pickles: Toss pickles with the remaining cornstarch. Whisk the egg in a shallow bowl. In a separate shallow bowl, add panko breadcrumbs. Dip pickles into egg, letting excess drip back, then dredge in panko, pressing to adhere. Heat ¼ inch of neutral oil in a skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Fry pickles until golden, about 2 minutes per side.
  4. Toast Buns: Discard all but 1 tbsp oil from the skillet. Heat the skillet over medium-high heat. Add buns, cut side down, and toast until lightly golden, 1–2 minutes. Transfer buns to plates. Divide the ground beef into 2 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook Burgers: Add 2 tsp oil to the skillet, then add patties. Top with half of the sliced onions and press with a spatula to flatten into 5-inch wide burgers. Cook, undisturbed, until browned on the bottom, 3–4 minutes. Flip burgers and cook until medium-rare, 2–3 minutes more (adjust for desired doneness).
  6. Finish & Serve: Mix ranch dressing with pickle brine in a small bowl. Toss fries with steak seasoning. Spread pickle ranch on the bottom buns, then layer with lettuce, burgers, raw onions, and fried pickles. Serve with seasoned fries and extra ranch for dipping. Enjoy!
