Smash Burger with Fried Pickles, Steak Fries & Pickled Ranch

Cuisine: American

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1/4 cup pickles (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large potatoes (cut into steak fries)
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 tbsp pickle juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Spread steak fries on a baking sheet, season with salt, and bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk ranch dressing with pickle juice to create pickled ranch. Set aside.
  3. Dip pickle slices into buttermilk, then dredge in a mixture of flour, cornmeal, salt, and pepper. Fry in a skillet with hot oil until golden and crispy.
  4. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. Form ground beef into thin patties and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, smashing them down for a crispy edge.
  5. Assemble burgers with buns, patties, fried pickles, and pickled ranch. Serve with steak fries. Enjoy!
