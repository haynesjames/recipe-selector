Meatloaf with Creamy Mustard Sauce & Buttery Tarragon Veggies

Cuisine: American

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp tarragon (fresh, chopped)
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, green beans, or similar)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into a loaf and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  5. While the meatloaf is baking, prepare the creamy mustard sauce: In a small saucepan, heat heavy cream over medium heat. Stir in Dijon mustard and tarragon. Simmer for 2-3 minutes and set aside.
  6. Steam or sauté your vegetables in butter with a pinch of salt and pepper until tender.
  7. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve slices of meatloaf with the mustard sauce drizzled over and buttery tarragon vegetables on the side. Enjoy!
