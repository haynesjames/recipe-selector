Air Fryer Salmon with Lemon Herb Quinoa and Parmesan Asparagus

Cuisine: American, Healthy

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Air Fryer Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic or garlic powder
  • Smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)
  • Tartar sauce (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400°F.
  2. Rub each fillet with olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon in the air fryer and cook for 7–8 minutes, depending on the thickness of the salmon. Time may vary between air fryers.
  4. Open the air fryer basket and check for desired doneness with a fork. Return to the air fryer for 1–2 minutes if needed.

Lemon Herb Quinoa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp fresh dill or parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Cook quinoa in broth according to package instructions.
  3. Once cooked, stir in olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Roasted Asparagus with Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread evenly on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast for 12–15 minutes, turning halfway through cooking.
  4. Sprinkle Parmesan over asparagus during the last 2 minutes of roasting. Serve immediately.
