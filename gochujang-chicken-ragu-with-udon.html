Gochujang Chicken Ragu with Udon, Cilantro & Scallions

Cuisine: Fusion (Korean-Italian)

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 3 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 scallions (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp cilantro (chopped)
  • 12 oz udon noodles
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook udon noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add garlic, ginger, and ground chicken. Cook until chicken is browned.
  3. Stir in gochujang, soy sauce, and chicken broth. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Toss the cooked noodles with the sauce until well-coated.
  5. Top with chopped scallions and cilantro before serving. Enjoy!
