Pan-Seared Rib-Eye Steaks Dinner

Cuisine: American

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Pan-Seared Rib-Eye Steaks

Ingredients

  • 2 rib-eye steaks (10–12 ounces each, boneless or bone-in)
  • 1 tbsp neutral-flavored oil (e.g., safflower oil, canola oil, grapeseed oil, or peanut oil)
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary

Instructions

  1. Prep the Steaks: Let the steaks sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  2. Season: Generously season both sides of the steaks with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the Skillet: Heat a heavy pan, such as a cast-iron skillet, over high heat until hot.
  4. Sear the Steaks: Add the oil to the skillet. Place the steaks in the pan in a single layer. Cook for 4–5 minutes per side, or until deep golden brown.
  5. Add Butter and Aromatics: Add the butter to the pan. Once melted, add the garlic cloves and rosemary. Stir to combine.
  6. Baste the Steaks: Spoon the butter mixture over the steaks repeatedly. Insert a probe thermometer into the thickest part of one of the steaks.
  7. Finish Cooking: Cook the steaks for an additional 2–3 minutes, or until the thermometer registers 130–135°F for medium doneness.
  8. Rest and Serve: Remove the steaks from the pan and let them rest for 5 minutes. Spoon the pan drippings over the steaks before slicing and serving.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¾ cup low-fat milk
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil Potatoes: Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20–25 minutes.
  2. Prepare Garlic Butter: Meanwhile, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat. Add the garlic and sauté until soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. Mash Potatoes: Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the garlic-butter mixture, milk, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine: Mash until smooth and creamy. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Serve: Garnish with additional parsley if desired. Enjoy!

Garlic Butter Sautéed Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Blanch Green Beans: Blanch the green beans in boiling water for 3 minutes.
  2. Sauté in Butter: Sauté the green beans in butter with garlic for 2–3 minutes, until tender-crisp.
  3. Season and Serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
