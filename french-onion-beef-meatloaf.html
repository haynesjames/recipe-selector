French Onion Beef Meatloaf with Creamed Peas and New Potatoes

Cuisine: American

Meal Type: Dinner

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup French onion soup mix
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb new potatoes (halved)
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste (for peas and potatoes)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, French onion soup mix, breadcrumbs, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into a loaf and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160째F (71째C).
  4. While the meatloaf is baking, prepare the creamed peas and new potatoes:
  5. Boil the new potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth, then slowly whisk in the milk. Cook until the mixture thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
  7. Add the peas to the sauce and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Combine the cooked potatoes with the creamed peas, season with salt and pepper to taste, and stir gently to coat.
  9. Serve slices of the meatloaf with creamed peas and new potatoes on the side. Enjoy!
